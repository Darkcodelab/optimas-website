Future of Risk: How AI Shapes Cognitive Risk Management with Aadesh and Sehrish

April 30, 2024

During the GISEC event, Aadesh engaged in a discussion with Sehrish, Assistant Editor at GEC Media Group, focusing on Understanding Cognitive Risk Management, elucidating its significance in today’s data-driven landscape and how AI amplifies the capacity to predict, monitor, and mitigate risks dynamically. They emphasized the advantages of automating risk management processes and how autonomous control systems can lead to faster response times and reduced human error. Additionally, they introduced Optimas, underscoring its mission to enhance digital security and risk management through AI-driven solutions, and delved into the product architecture of Optimas, unveiling its framework and functionalities. This conversation underscores the transformative potential of cognitive approaches and AI-driven solutions in revolutionizing risk management practices.
