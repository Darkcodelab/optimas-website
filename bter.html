The framework

The BTERTM framework is meticulously designed to encompass all facets of technology risk management and resilience building. Here's a glimpse into the core components that make BTERTM uniquely effective

BTERTM ASSURANCE & COMMUNICATE:

  • Exposure Clarity: Provides clear insights into technology risks.
  • Executive Reporting: Delivers exposure reports for leadership.
  • Future-Ready Predictions: Enables forward-looking risk anticipation

BTERTM PREDICTION & DETECTION :

  • Forecasting: Predicts threats using advanced analytics powered by AI
  • Real-time Alerts: Identifies emerging risks instantly.
  • Comprehensive Risk View: Merges external and internal data for a full risk landscape.

BTERTM GOVERNANCE & MANAGE :

  • Policy Alignment: Matches technology use with business goals.
  • Regulatory Compliance: Ensures adherence to laws and standards.
  • Lifecycle Management: Oversees risk from identification to mitigation.

BTERTM PREVENTION & RESPONSE :

  • Mitigation Strategies: Implements actions to reduce risk exposure.
  • Incident Plans: Offers clear protocols for swift incident response.
  • Builds Resilience: Enhances recovery and defense against future threats.

BTERTM CORE :

  • Asset Tracking: Identifies and manages technology assets.
  • Risk Context: Analyzes the business environment for better security.
  • Threat Analysis: Evaluates potential dangers and weaknesses.
  • Predictive Tools: Uses simulations for planning and risk assessment.
